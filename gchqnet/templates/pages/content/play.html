The Great Camp Hexpansion Quest (GCHQ.NET) is a distributed interactive game for attendees of Electromagnetic Field 2024.

We've hidden nearly 100 hexpansions around site, waiting to be connected to your badge. When you connect a hexpansion to your badge you collect points for that location.

From points you get a position on our leaderboard - now you can play against everyone else at EMF! If you want to compete play along with your friends, you can set up a private leaderboard for yourselves.
